The problem · 01[ 02 / 06 ]

Ctrl+F finds words.
Not answers.

Contracts, research papers, reports, manuals — the knowledge is right there.

But search matches strings, not meaning. So we skim, we miss the clause that mattered, and we re-ask questions someone already answered on page 30.
